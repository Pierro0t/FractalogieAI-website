Features

Everything is built to reveal one thing

Not a feed, not a feature list to manage. Every part of Fractalogie points the same direction — toward the single loop quietly running your life, and the structure underneath it.

01 — The scan

The guided scan

A calm, live conversation with a mirror that asks one destabilising question at a time.

No flood of prompts. No advice. No twenty-field questionnaire. Just one precise thread, followed all the way down — until the trigger, your reaction, the emotion it protects, and the price you keep paying come into focus on their own.

◆ trigger ◆ reaction ◆ emotion ◆ the price
02 — The Mirror

One clear revelation

Each scan returns a single, crisp insight — not a feed to scroll.

The Mirror names a repetition you hadn't put into words. One sentence that holds the loop: “When ___, I ___, because I feel ___, and it leads to ___.” You don't leave with homework. You leave seeing something you can't unsee.

✦ one insight per scan ✦ no infinite feed
03 — The map

Your fractal map

Every loop you decode traces back to one primary attractor.

Relationships, money, work, the body — they look like separate problems, but the map shows them running the same structure. Not many problems. One, repeating, at different scales. Seeing them connect is where the picture stops being abstract.

⟡ relationships ⟡ money ⟡ work ⟡ body
04 — Evolution

The architecture of who you're becoming

Saved motifs build a living, evolving picture of your structure over time.

Each revelation you keep adds a line to the drawing. Months in, you're not looking at scattered notes — you're looking at the shape of yourself, how it shifts, where it loosens. The architecture isn't fixed. It's becoming, and you get to watch.

∞ saved motifs ∞ evolving over time
05 — Beyond the app

Beyond the app

When seeing isn't enough, a private 1:1 reconstruction reorganises the identity generating the loop.

The app reveals the structure. The reconstruction is where it gets rebuilt — at the root, in a private session with a real human. For loops that keep repeating even after you've named them, and you're ready to change the self-definition holding them in place.

◇ private 1:1 ◇ at the root
By design

Quiet guarantees

The things we chose not to build are part of the product. No camera, no ads, no data brokers — just a mirror you can trust.

⌨

Text only

No camera, no photos, no microphone. Fractalogie reads only what you choose to write.

⌖

No trackers, no ads

No analytics SDKs, no ad networks, no data brokers. Nothing follows you out of the app.

◷

Works without an account

The core experience runs with no sign-in. An account only adds optional cloud sync.

⌫

Deletion in one tap

Erase your account and every piece of cloud data instantly, from inside the app.

⟡

EN · FR · ES · DE

The interface and every analysis follow your chosen language — English, French, Spanish, German.

⊘

Never trains AI models

Your words are used to generate your analysis — never to train models, ours or anyone's.
