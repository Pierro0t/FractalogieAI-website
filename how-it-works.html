How it works

How a loop comes undone

One situation. A few minutes. One thread, followed all the way down — until the structure quietly running your life becomes something you can finally see.

No account needed · text only · no tracking

The scan

Four steps, one loop

You don't fill in forms or pick from menus. You have a short, precise conversation with a mirror that only ever follows one thread.

01

Name it

Choose a situation that keeps happening — the argument, the exit, the silence — and quietly holds you back.

02

Describe once

Tell it about one real, recent moment it happened. No theory, no diagnosis — just your own words.

03

Be mirrored

A live AI asks one precise question at a time, surfacing the trigger, your reaction, the emotion, and the price.

04

See the structure

The loop crystallises into a single motif sentence — and the hidden logic that has been keeping it running.

The conversation

A live mirror, one question at a time

This isn't a chatbot that talks at you. It's a mirror. Calm and precise, it reads what you wrote and reflects it back — then asks the single question that goes one layer deeper.

It never floods you with options. It never moralises or hands out advice. It asks one thing, waits, and follows your answer — destabilising in the gentlest way, because it only ever names what is already there.

  • One question at a time — never a wall of text
  • Calm and precise, never clinical or cold
  • No advice, no judgement, no moralising
  • Follows your thread — never changes the subject
The revelation

One clear insight, not a feed

When the thread is complete, the mirror returns a single, crisp revelation — not an endless feed to scroll. It puts into one sentence a repetition you'd felt for years but never quite named.

You see the fractal motif, the hidden logic underneath, the reversal that loosens its grip, and where it sits on your personal map — the structure beneath relationships, money, work and body.

  • Your fractal motif, in a single sentence
  • The hidden logic the loop protects
  • The reversal that destabilises it
  • Its place on your personal map
