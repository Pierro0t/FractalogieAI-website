Questions

Good to know

Is Fractalogie therapy?＋
No. Fractalogie is a tool for self-reflection — not therapy, medical care, or mental-health treatment. It doesn't diagnose or treat anything, and it's not a substitute for a qualified professional. If you're in distress, please contact a professional or your local emergency number.
What happens to what I write?＋
Your scan text is sent securely to OpenAI's API to generate your analysis, and is never used to train models. Without an account it stays on your device; with an account it's stored privately (Supabase) so it syncs across devices. We sell nothing and run no tracking. See our Privacy Policy.
Do I need an account?＋
No. The core of the app works without one. Signing in (Apple or Google) simply enables cloud sync. You can delete your account and all cloud data in one tap from the app.
Which platforms and languages?＋
Fractalogie is an iOS app, available in English, French, Spanish and German. The interface and every analysis follow your chosen language.
How does billing work?＋
Your first scan is free. Founding Access is a monthly auto-renewing subscription purchased through Apple; the price is shown before you buy. It renews unless cancelled at least 24h before the period ends — manage it anytime in your App Store settings.

Fractalogie is for self-reflection only. It is not medical, psychological, or professional advice, and not a crisis service. If you may be in danger, contact your local emergency number (112 in the EU, 988 in the US) or a qualified professional.
