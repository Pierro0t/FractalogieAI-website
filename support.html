Support

We usually reply within 48 hours.

Need a hand, found a bug, or have a question? Email us at contact@fractalogie.ai and we'll get back to you. Below are answers to the most common questions.

Getting started

Open the app, choose a recurring situation, describe one recent occurrence, and answer the mirror's questions one at a time. After a few exchanges your loop crystallises into a fractal motif. Your first scan is free and needs no account.

Managing your subscription

Founding Access is a monthly auto-renewing subscription billed through Apple. To view, change or cancel it: open the Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Fractalogie. Cancelling at least 24 hours before the renewal date stops the next charge; you keep access until the end of the current period.

Restoring a purchase

If you reinstalled the app or switched devices, open the paywall and tap “restore purchases” to re-activate your membership on the same Apple ID.

Refunds

Purchases are processed by Apple, so refunds are handled by Apple. Request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Deleting your account & data

In the app, go to Me → delete my account. This permanently erases your account and every scan stored in the cloud, immediately. Scans stored only on your device are removed when you delete the app.

Privacy

We sell nothing and run no tracking. Your scan text is processed by OpenAI's API to generate your analysis and is never used to train models. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

A note on safety

Fractalogie is a tool for self-reflection, not therapy or a crisis service. If you are in distress or may be in danger, please contact a qualified professional or your local emergency number (112 in the EU, 988 in the US) right away.
